// Random Tempo Generator

A practice tool for musicians. Generate random tempos to sharpen your sight-reading, improve your internal clock, and break out of comfort-zone BPMs.

Random Tempos

Generate random BPM values within your chosen range. Perfect for sight-reading practice and building tempo awareness.

Custom Range

Set your own minimum and maximum BPM range to target specific tempos for your practice sessions.

Tap-to-Generate

Simple, distraction-free interface. Tap to generate a new random tempo instantly during your practice.

Clean Design

Minimal, focused UI that stays out of your way. No ads, no distractions — just you and the tempo.

Built for Musicians

Designed by a working musician who understands the demands of practice, rehearsal, and performance.

Privacy First

No data collection, no tracking, no accounts required. Your practice stays private.
